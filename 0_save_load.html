How to save and load Snap! projects

  1. If you haven't already opened Snap, open it now: http://snap.berkeley.edu/run

  2. Click the button:

  3. If you see "Logout username", that means you're logged into your account already.

  4. Otherwise, click "Login..." or "Signup..." and follow the instructions.

  5. To save or load your work, click the button and choose "Save", "Save As...", or "Open...".
