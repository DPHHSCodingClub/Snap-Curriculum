How to save and load Replit projects

  1. If you haven't already opened Replit, open it now: https://replit.com/

  2. If you are not automatically logged in, enter your login details and click "Log In" or click "Sign up" and follow the instructions.

    If you open someone else's replit project and want to fork it (make a copy for yourself), you'll have to click "Log In" or "Sign Up" and follow the instructions.

  3. Your projects are saved automatically every time you press the button at the top of the screen. If you want to load a project, click the menu button and select "My Repls"

  4. If you want to load a project from your main account page, find "My Repls" on the menu to the left of the screen. If you do not see this menu, try clicking the menu button on the top left first.
