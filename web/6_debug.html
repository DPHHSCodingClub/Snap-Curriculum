Debug It!

Instead of making your own project, you will fix some projects that have some coding errors, which are called bugs. The process of finding and fixing these errors is called debugging. Can you find and squash all the bugs?

  1. Bronze-level debugging. Visit replit.com/@atr6/Debug-Task-1 and click the "Fork" button to make your own copy.
